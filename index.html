Sisi Arundathee

To raise the funds for the project, we organize "Sisi Arundathee", the ultimate cultural extravaganza of the year with the participation of undergraduates of Faculty of Applied Sciences of University of Sri Jayewardenepura. Sisi Arundathee is a divine platform to the undergraduates to discover and enlighten their hidden innermost talents in front of the biggest audience on the glamorous stage on Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH) every year.

Sip Sayura

Every year as the annual Cooperate Social Responsibility (sisi-arundathee) project, the Students' Council of the Faculty of Applied Sciences, University of Sri Jayewardenepura organize "Sip Sayura", incorporate with the lecturers, undergraduates of Faculty of Applied Sciences and our valuable sponsors from Corporate Sector. Through this project we donate a fully equipped computer laboratory to a rural school in our country to get forward the school students with the knowledge of technology.

About

Sisi Arundathee is renowned as the most prestigious cultural extravaganza organized by the Students’ council of Faculty of Applied Sciences, of University of Sri Jayewardenepura. The birth of this marvellous event took place in the year 2001 where it marked a turning point of young scientists studying in the faculty. Discovering and being enlightened by their hidden innermost talents, those young scientists then, created a spark and named it “Sisi Arundathee”. That little spark, over the years has turned to be a blazing flame in every batch of the faculty, to continue the tradition with great care and sacrifice annually.
