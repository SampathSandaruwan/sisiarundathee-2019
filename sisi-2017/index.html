Sisi Arundathee 2017

Sisi Arundathee 2017 was held on 8th August 2017 at Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH) for the 16th consecutive time with the glamorous performances of undergraduates of Faculty of Applied Sciences.

Crescent foods was our major partner in Sisi Arundathee 2017, People's Bank as our official banking partner and Sri Lanka Rupawahini Cooperation as our official media partner joined hands with us for success the event.

The funds mounted from Sisi Arundathee 2017 was directed to the annual Cooperate Social Responsibility (CSR) project "Sip Sayura" organized by the Students' Council of Faculty of Applied Sciences, University of Sri Jayewardenepura which was donated a fully equipped computer laboratory to rural area school in Sri Lanka.
